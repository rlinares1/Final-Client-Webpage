The San Diego Chicken

Who is he?

The San Diego Chicken began his reign when he was created as a cartoon character for KGB-FM Radio. A man named Ted Giannoulas became the mascot for the San Diego Padres in March 1974. The mascot is known for being one of the most controversal and one of the funniest mascots of any baseball team.

"It's not the end, but I can see it from here" (Ted Giannoulas)

The San Diego Chicken has done various pranks and the mascot usually interrupts some games with these pranks. The mascot is known to visit players and umpires in the middle of games, distracting them. The chicken also has his trademark dance moves that he is also largely known for. There was a case where when Ted Giannoulas retired, he sued the new guy in the mascot costume for stealing his dance moves.
